India Gate

India Gate is a war memorial built for soldiers of British Indian Army personnel who died during the First world -war during 1914 – 1918. 

The architecture here is often compared to Arc De Triomphe in Paris and Gateway of India in Mumbai.

More Places

1.Qutab Minar, made of red stone and marble is the main attraction in India. It’s the part of Qutab Complex which is declared as a UNESCO world heritage site 

2.Lotus Temple in Delhi is recognized as the most visited building due to its architectural design.  It is built like a Lotus flower using white marble with 27 free-standing marble-clad petals on nine sides.
3.Jama Masjid, also called Masjid-I Jahan-Numa is one of the largest mosques in India built by Shah Jahan between 1644 and 1656.
